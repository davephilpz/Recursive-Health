1. What is being healthy in the first place?

In my personal definition, being healthy is the ability to live your life to the fullest without losing performance of losing certain body functionality. This can mean different things to different people, but I would consider it not having any genetic or lifestyle diseases that hinder your daily life or any physical or mental activity that you engage in.

2. What areas should I focus on to get or stay healthy?

This section will cover key areas you can focus on the get the most out of your body. Remeber that your body is a machine and that a machine without proper fuel and building blocks cannot properly function. This guide is setup in a hierarchical format and you can use this to drill down to the details for the section you wish to learn more about.

Key areas of focus for good health:

  1. Sleep: This is time where your body repairs itself, your immune system builds itself up, your muscles get stronger and your brain organizes thought. Failing to get proper sleep, mainly deep sleep and REM sleep will cause all other areas to fail and cannot be ignored.
    Improve your sleep now!
  2. Diet: This is probably the next most important thing after sleep as your body is a machine and needs the properfuel and building blocks in order to function as God intended it to. Good food is hard to come by these days and is the cause of most modern day Western culture diseases.
    Improve your diet now!
  3. Stress management: When people hear stress most if not all only think about mental stress. All forms of mental stress, sickness, exercise, excessive hot/cold, low oxygen, lack of food, water, or electrolytes etc. are all forms of stress that damage the body. Lucky for us is that if we manage these stresses and keep them acute plus rest, the body will come back stronger that it was before. Failing to manage stress will lead to sickness and possibly disease or even death. These types of stress are also known as hormesis.
    Manage your stress now!
  4. Posture: Posture is important because not being in proper posture for long periods of time can and will hinder you body from performing the way it was intended to. Proper posture cannot be maintained without properly maintaining bones, ligaments, tendons and muscles which give us our posture in the first place.
    Improve your posture now!

This cycle is typically repeated for three to four rounds. Practitioners of the method report feelings of euphoria, calm, and increased tolerance to cold temperatures after performing these breathing exercises.

3. Deep diving into health

This website is dedicated to helping people for FREE and the main purpose is to provide enough information as an entryway into various sectors of health and wellness. It is a collection of publically available information on health from a plethora of medical professionals, medical journals and personal experimentation and experience. Just drill down to the level of detail you are interested in. New content will slowly be added and old content updated if later new studies and experiments overturn the facts.

Please also be aware that everyone has a slightly different body makeup and different genes that can affect how different factors effect your health. Even though lifestyle choices are the majority of health (80-90% according to some leading experts Like David Sinclair), genes can disallow you from performing certain activities or hinder your health in some ways. Some people also have pre-existing medical conditions all of which are most likely lifestyle illnesses and can be reversed. Remeber, there is always a cure and that knowing is half the battle!

This guide is meant to administer the red pill and point people in the right direction to live a happy, healthly life and extend their healthy lifespan. Be sure to consult a medical professional before attempting to try anything listed on this website or in any of the videos or other material that is linked herewithin.
