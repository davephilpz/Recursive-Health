2. Why I started a health blog

Back in 2020 I was stuck at home a lot and also having some postural issues of my own I decided to start studying anything health related that I could get my hands on.

Key areas of focus for good health:

  1. Sleep: This is time where your body repairs itself, your immune system builds itself up, your muscles get stronger and your brain organizes thought. Failing to get proper sleep, mainly deep sleep and REM sleep will cause all other areas to fail and cannot be ignored.
    Improve your sleep now!
  2. Diet: This is probably the next most important thing after sleep as your body is a machine and needs the properfuel and building blocks in order to function as God intended it to. Good food is hard to come by these days and is the cause of most modern day Western culture diseases.
    Improve your diet now!
  3. Stress management: When people hear stress most if not all only think about mental stress. All forms of mental stress, sickness, exercise, excessive hot/cold, low oxygen, lack of food, water, or electrolytes etc. are all forms of stress that damage the body. Lucky for us is that if we manage these stresses and keep them acute plus rest, the body will come back stronger that it was before. Failing to manage stress will lead to sickness and possibly disease or even death. These types of stress are also known as hormesis.
    Manage your stress now!
  4. Posture: Posture is important because not being in proper posture for long periods of time can and will hinder you body from performing the way it was intended to. Proper posture cannot be maintained without properly maintaining bones, ligaments, tendons and muscles which give us our posture in the first place.
    Improve your posture now!

This cycle is typically repeated for three to four rounds. Practitioners of the method report feelings of euphoria, calm, and increased tolerance to cold temperatures after performing these breathing exercises.
